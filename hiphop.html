VSU Modern is a competitive dance team at UCLA established in 2006. NSU Modern
NSU Modern is one of UCLA's premiere hip-hop dance teams and is home to some of the university's most versatile and talented dancers. Its members are proud to represent the Nikkei Student Union, UCLA's only Japanese American student association. Comprised of multi-ethnic Bruins from various dance backgrounds, NSU Modern strives to perform a variety of dance styles.
